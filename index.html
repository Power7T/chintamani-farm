Our Story

A Farm That Feels Like Home

Tucked away in the peaceful village of Thal, Alibaug, Chintamani Farm is a cherished family retreat surrounded by lush greenery and nature. The air smells of earth and ocean — because the beach is just minutes away.

We opened our farm to guests who crave genuine peace: no resort crowds, no city noise. Just home-cooked food, warm family hospitality, and the sound of birds in the morning.
